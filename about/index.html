About me

I’m a self-taught, coffee-infused idea generator. Computer is my first language, English is my second.


On this website I write about my journeys through life as a young technology evangelist.. whether they be ideas I’ve had, apps I’ve created, or quotes that inspire me.

History

  • Born April 2nd 1989 in St. Peters, Missouri.
  • Bought my first computer with my saved allowance when I was 12. Proceeded to install Photoshop on it.
  • Taught myself how to build websites for the WORLD WIDE WEB.
  • Followed the pack and went to college.. didn’t last long.
  • Interned at Jordan Lawrence as a junior web developer.
  • Joined Foundry Software for my first full-time gig, built websites for a variety of clients.
  • Moved to Cat5 Commerce to help transition their ecommerce platform to a responsive design framework.
  • Became a husband to a beautiful wife and father to a beautiful dog on October 31, 2015.
  • Began 2016 with a new company, joined New Balance as a senior developer.

Trivia

  • I read as much as I can; typically books on unconventional entrepreneurs.
  • I love coffee.. too much.
  • Hockey is my stress-reliever and stress-inducer.
  • I’m inspired by Tim Ferriss, Kevin Rose, and Oliver Emberton.
